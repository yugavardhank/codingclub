Mission of the Coding Club

Dedicated to enhancing the coding culture on campus, we are actively engaged in organizing regular coding classes, coding contests, and informative sessions aimed at fostering a more intellectually stimulating and technically proficient environment.

Vision of the Coding Club

To foster robust community development within the realm of coding, with the overarching goal of making substantial contributions across diverse fields, while steadfastly upholding the principles of integrity in TAU.

Focus of the Coding Club

To enhance the coding culture within the University, it is proposed to organize periodic lectures encompassing a spectrum of programming proficiency levels, ranging from foundational concepts to advanced topics. Additionally, sessions on supplementary skills shall be incorporated to augment the overall development of participants.

Objectives of the Coding Club

  • Promote excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education and overall practice.

  • Advance the development and exchange of relevant knowledge.

  • Gain technical and collaborative skills to design real-life projects.

  • Be a forum for interaction among students of the institute.

  • Learn steps of the engineering design cycle (discover- evaluate, design, develop evaluate, deliver- evaluate) and how it works as a practical problem-solving method.
